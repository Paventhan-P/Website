Overview

Intigriti is a leading crowdsourced security platform, simplifying the process for companies to obtain comprehensive security assessments through their platform. I joined at Intigriti as a security researcher with the goal of identifying vulnerabilities in client products.

On a large team of independent hackers, I worked directly under the independent researcher.

My Role

Responsible for the security chapter, I oversee researchers from various areas within the organization, covering everything from OWASP Top 10 vulnerabilities to platform bugs.

I actively contribute to multiple programs and have successfully discovered and validated numerous bugs. As part of my contributions, I have implemented a robust bug validation process, ensuring that only genuine vulnerabilities are reported and addressed.

Through my expertise and dedication, I strive to enhance the security posture of our organization and deliver reliable and trustworthy results
