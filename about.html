About

"I work for & with disruptive companies to secure their organization with their technical teams. By creating information, I help others in developing their careers in security."

My name is Paventhan, and I live in Chennai, a city in India.

I work for & with disruptive companies with their product and website vulnerabilities as well as with building a strong internal team by working for and with them. I pursue bug bounty hunting in addition to my full-time job to carry out this task. Also, I work with ambitious person to grow their careers in cyber security.

I spent over a month at the India’s “most desirable startup” Dream Aerospace, as an Design Engineer in their development department, building combustion chamber for their own rocket propulsion system.

I was a graduate engineer in aeronautical engineering with expertise as a design engineer prior to when I became a security engineer & bug bounty hunter. Due to the fact that I have spent the most of my career focusing on securing the website and mobile application, My technical interests/strengths are vulnerability assessment and penetration testing.
