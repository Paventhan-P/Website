Working as a White Hat security researcher for Web3 at Immunefi

Overview

I started working with Immunefi as a security researcher. They were looking for an independent security researcher to assist them in identifying a vulnerability in their client's product.

My Role

I initially started by testing the website of immunefi clients products within the assets in scope. These assets played a crucial role in determining the bountiesfor the products, including setting limits and identifying potential threats.

I did a few of smart contract audits that have helped me understand the blockchain network concept and gained more knowledge about web3 security.

Through my journey with immunefi, I accepted the responsibility to secure the web3 technologyfor the blockchain network, which helped me build a track record in the security industry. This included protecting the network and guiding me through the technology.
