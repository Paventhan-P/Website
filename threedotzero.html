Work  ✺  Content Creation  ✺  Mentorship

Threedotzero is a company I own, with a mission of helping as many individuals as possible to succeed in their professions.

There are three areas of focus for ThreeDotZero:

Threedotzero Substack - This is a regular newsletter where I share technique and growth ideas that are both actionable and sustainable for security professionals of all levels, from entry-level workers to those in mid-level their positions.

Threedotzero Podcast - This is where we explore the rapidly changing field of cybersecurity. I may discuss interesting debates, emerging trends, and expert interviews that offer valuable information and useful advice to keep you knowledgeable, empowered, and one step ahead in the field of digital security.

Threedotzero Advising - This is specifically for those wanting to collaborate with an established business that wants to assist in resolving certain issues. whether an individual, a team, or technical team.

Why?

I set up threedotzero in 2023 to focus on helping engineers and engineering teams flourish.

As a career changer, I moved into Security industry completely self taught at the age of 21. I grew fairly quickly through the titles, and went on to land what I would have considered at the time my dream job.

What got me there was my technical ability. But the fact I was fortunate enough to have picked up vital soft skills from my previous roles outside of tech, as well as having several good mentors.

As time has progressed, much of the content focused on building a career and growing as an engineer is focused on the mid-level+ levels. I believe that these lessons apply to everyone in tech, and shouldn't just be taught to those who are most senior.

Not everyone is fortunate enough to have a manager focusing purely on their growth at work. So I wanted to share what I had learned through my career, as well as share what I would talk about to direct reports in mentoring & Advising sessions with everyone who cares to read.
