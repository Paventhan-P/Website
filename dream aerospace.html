Dream Aerospace

Work  ✺  Engineering  ✺  Design

Overview

Dream Aerospace is a Indian based 'Aerospace Startup', has emerged as a competitor to the small-scale industry rocket sector, prioritizing a user-friendly approach and product-centric mindset. Remarkably, within just one year of its inception, the startup successfully developed a KN impulse engine, reflecting their exceptionalcapabilities.

I applied to the startup in 2023, and was fortunate enough to earn a spot on the engineering design team.

My Role

My role was to design and analysis, calculate the value for design configuration, and confirm with various other parts to make sure the calculated value matched the required value and was stable in all aspects of aerodynamics.

I worked on the design for the combustion chamber, that had an impact on the rocket's propulsion system.

Redesigning the propulsion system to make it simple to remove and fix system components and developing it from a cubsat to a 30 kg rocket allowed the majority of students and private individuals launch their rockets.
